Githunguri Psycho

Drawing from my deep experience as a graphic designer, I crafted promotional materials for the chilling short film, "Githunguri Psycho" set in the haunting ambiance of Githunguri. Integrating visual storytelling techniques, I mirrored the film's intertwining of myths and grim realities within the poster's design. Utilizing a carefully selected color palette, I echoed the narrative's dread and suspense, complemented by typography that resonates with its haunting undertones. Through balanced composition, I emphasized Githunguri's eerie character, ensuring every intricate detail captured the gripping tension of the film, making it an irresistible lure for potential viewers.
